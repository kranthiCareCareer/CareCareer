### Audit Policy

- Denials: persistently audited in `identity.authorization_decisions`
- Allows: structured telemetry only (not persisted)
- Outbox: NOT required for decisions (not domain events)
- Infrastructure failures: always deny, never allow
- Documented in: docs/security/AUTHORIZATION-DECISION-AUDIT-POLICY.md

### Service Ownership

- Endpoint: identity-service
- ADR: docs/architecture/decisions/ADR-authorization-decision-service-ownership.md
- Rationale: owns users, memberships, roles, permissions, sessions
